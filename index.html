Products

What We Create

Silkys

A silky is a security blanket for infants and children. It is soft flannel on one side and satin on the other. It is trimmed in satin trim. They are colorful and usually have a theme print. Research shows that the silky will retain the mother's "scent" and the baby can be comforted by the silky. Most can be embroider with the child's name and birthdate. Nana's silkys make great baby shower gifts!

Cupcake Gifts

A Cupcake Gift is a gift card holder made of ribbon and paper. It looks like a cupcake and even has a cherry on top. The cherry is actually a placeholder for a gift card. These special treats can even be made to store candy in the bottom. They come in large and small sizes. The small comes in it's own box! There is a theme for every occasion. Amy's Cupcake Gifts make great graduation presents!

Guitars

A guitar is a wonderful instrument but only when it is proper working order. Most need constant work and Jonathan provides a great service. He can change strings, tune and even rebuild a broken one. He loves to tinker and take them apart. He gives each one a gentle cleaning and take great pride in his work.

Woodworking

Working with wood is a dirty job. Most people don't like the smell of sawdust or even to get the tools out. Scott loves to create patterns and prints. He can design some things with a few details and cut them out. He have a great workshop and would love to help create a special gift or keepsake for you.
